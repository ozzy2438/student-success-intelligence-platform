# Stakeholder Map

| Stakeholder | Primary need | Likely concern | Design response |
|---|---|---|---|
| Chief Data and Analytics Officer | Trusted, reusable decision-support capability | Fragmented metrics and poor adoption | Governed semantic model, documented KPIs and executive dashboard |
| COO / Executive leadership | Enterprise risk and service-demand visibility | Information overload | KPI-first overview with clear action narrative |
| Faculty leaders | Course and cohort visibility | Being held accountable for poorly contextualised metrics | Drill-down, trend views and documented limitations |
| Course coordinators | Assessment and engagement insight | Misinterpretation of student activity | Course-level aggregation, explanatory context and no automated action |
| Student-support managers | Prioritised workload | Too many false positives | Capacity-aware threshold and human review workflow |
| Student-support advisers | Actionable student context | Stigma and unsafe intervention | Restricted access, transparent reason codes and support-first recommendations |
| Students | Fair, respectful and beneficial use of data | Surveillance, misclassification or adverse consequences | Transparency, minimal data use, human oversight and support-only purpose |
| Privacy and governance teams | Compliance and auditability | Excessive collection, disclosure or secondary use | De-identification, classification, lineage, access matrix and model card |
| Data engineering / IT | Stable, maintainable delivery | Unreliable manual processes | Reproducible scripts, dbt transformations, tests and runbook |

## Stakeholder engagement cadence

- **Weekly during pilot:** analytics delivery team, student support, faculty representative
- **Monthly:** data governance, privacy, technology and operational sponsors
- **Per term:** model monitoring, cohort review, KPI review and student feedback loop
